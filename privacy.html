Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of Linxella ("we" or "us"). We are committed to ensuring the utmost privacy and security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information. By using our services and visiting Linxella.io ("our website"), you agree to the practices detailed in this policy.

If you have inquiries about this privacy policy or the security of your personal information, feel free to contact us at support@linxella.io.

Kindly read this Privacy Policy carefully. By engaging with our services, you consent to the utilization of your personal data in line with our Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Policy. By implementing and adhering to this Privacy Policy, we strive to enhance your online experience by ensuring its security and enjoyment.

Please note that your use of our website is subject to our Terms of Use, and any services provided are subject to our Terms and Conditions. Your visit to our website signifies your acceptance and consent to the practices described herein.

Various laws dictate the appropriate handling of personal data, which refers to information identifying individuals. We hold a serious commitment to fulfilling these obligations by storing and processing personal data responsibly and ethically.

2. Purpose of a Privacy Policy

Various laws dictate the appropriate handling of personal data, which refers to information identifying individuals. We hold a serious commitment to fulfilling these obligations by storing and processing personal data responsibly and ethically.

We prioritize collecting, storing, and processing only the necessary personal data required for our services. This may encompass details like your name, address, contact information, and Anti Money Laundering / Know Your Customer (AML/KYC) related data.

Additionally, our website's servers automatically collect and record information about site visitors. More information on cookies can be found in our Cookie Policy.

We collect your personal data upon your full registration with us, excluding the automatically gathered information detailed above.

3. Collection of Personal Data

  • We collect your personal data through various means, including:
  • Communication with us (e.g., registering for our services)
  • Interaction with our products or services
  • Participation in competitions or promotions
  • Making payments or modifying account details
  • Visiting our website (e.g., through cookies and technical information)

4. Use of Personal Data

  • We retain and use your personal data for several primary purposes, including:
  • Assessing suitability for products and services
  • Conducting AML/KYC assessments and investor risk profiling
  • Improving our products and services through market research
  • Preventing, detecting, investigating, and prosecuting crimes
  • Complying with legal obligations and agreements
  • Providing services, contacting you, and maintaining accounts
  • Enhancing website security and presenting relevant content
  • Administering the site and generating statistics
  • Additionally, with your consent, we may inform you about relevant products and services.

5. Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your personal information and have invested resources to safeguard it. Our external service providers adhere to the same standards. While the Internet isn't entirely secure, we take necessary precautions, including SSL and TLS technology. You can also contribute to data security by following recommended guidelines.

6. Data Retention

We retain financial transaction information for the required period under tax and revenue laws. Profile and membership data are retained as long as you are an active member and up to 10 years after membership cessation. Other data is erased or anonymized when no longer needed for its intended purpose.

7. Disclosure of Personal Data

We collaborate with third-party service providers to process personal data related to their services. These providers may operate in different countries. While our Privacy Policy doesn't apply to third-party websites or their practices, we aim for high privacy standards.

8. Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw data processing consent at any time, including marketing preferences. Instructions are provided in our communications or by contacting us. Profiling is done for site analysis and improvement, with no other automated decision-making.

9. Monitoring

We may record communications to ensure compliance with legal obligations and internal policies

10. Your Rights

Under relevant data protection laws, you have various rights, including access, correction, erasure, restriction, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@linxella.io.

11. Third-Party Websites and Social Media

Our website may link to third-party sites, and we have social media channels. We don't control their policies or practices.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at our discretion. Regular reviews are recommended.

13. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at support@linxella.io. If dissatisfied, you can contact the relevant privacy supervisory authority.
